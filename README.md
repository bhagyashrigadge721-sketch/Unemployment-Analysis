# Unemployment Analysis with Python 📊

This project is completed as part of my Data Science Internship Task 2 at **SAM AI Technologies**. The objective is to analyze the unemployment rate in India, investigate the direct impact of the Covid-19 pandemic, and discover seasonal/regional patterns in the workforce dataset.

## 📈 Key Insights Discovered
1. **Covid-19 Impact:** A massive surge in the estimated unemployment rate was observed immediately following the nationwide lockdown declared in March 2020.
2. **Rural vs Urban:** Urban sectors faced a wider range of structural unemployment fluctuations compared to rural areas.
3. **Hardest Hit States:** Tripura and Haryana recorded the highest average unemployment percentages during this period.

## 🛠️ Data Pipeline & Workflow
- **Data Cleaning:** Handled hidden column spaces and dropped complete structural null blocks.
- **Time-Series Parsing:** Parsed text dates into structured pandas datetime formats to group tracking by Months and Years.
- **Visualizations:** Created unified timeline trend lines, box-spread distributions, and monthly categorical bar charts using Seaborn.
